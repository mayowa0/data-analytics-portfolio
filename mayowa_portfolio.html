I'm a data analyst and researcher with a foundation in Materials & Metallurgical Engineering from the University of Ilorin. My engineering mindset — systematic, evidence-based, optimization-driven — is exactly what I now apply to data. I transitioned into data analytics because I recognized that the most impactful lever in any organization is the quality of its decisions, and decisions live or die on data.

At The Dataseed Company (SeedFi), I build and maintain SQL pipelines, have worked on automating data workflows with Python, and create interactive Looker Studio dashboards that give business teams a clear, real-time picture of performance. I also led a full Q1 business performance analysis, producing a slide deck that translated raw operational data into strategic recommendations for leadership.

Alongside my industry work, I'm an active researcher with two peer-reviewed publications and a manuscript under review — spanning sustainable energy, materials science, and circular economy. I bring this same research rigor to every dataset I work with.

I'm actively seeking opportunities to bring this blend of technical depth, analytical rigor, and clear communication to data-driven roles — helping organizations make smarter, faster decisions through the power of analytics and data.

Research · Materials Science
Mineralogical Characterization of Lithium-Bearing Ores
Conducted a detailed mineralogical study of lithium ore samples from Sooro and Zankan Pits 1 & 2 using advanced analytical techniques — SEM-EDS, XRD, optical petrography, and microscopy. Machine Learning · Fintech
Loan Default Prediction Model
Built a binary classification model to predict loan default risk using borrower demographic, financial, and behavioral features. Compared Logistic Regression, Random Forest, and XGBoost — achieving 89% accuracy and a 0.91 ROC-AUC score. Applied SMOTE to handle class imbalance and generated feature importance outputs to make predictions explainable to non-technical stakeholders.

EDA · Data Visualization · Energy
Nigeria Energy Access Analysis
A data-driven exploration of electricity access disparities across Nigeria’s six geopolitical zones using public data from the World Bank, NERC, and the Nigerian Bureau of Statistics. Analysed trends from 2010–2023, mapped the 51-percentage-point gap between the South West and North East, and visualized the chronic generation-vs-demand deficit that leaves over 85 million Nigerians without reliable power.

07
Excel · Tableau · Sustainability
CO₂ Emissions & Recycling Impact Dashboard
Inspired by my published research on metallurgical recycling, this project quantifies the environmental benefit of metal recycling across five material streams — aluminium, steel, copper, lead, and e-waste. Built a dynamic Excel impact calculator and a 4-page Tableau dashboard tracking CO₂ savings, energy reduction, and recovery rates. Key finding: full formalization of Lagos’s recycling sector could avoid 248,000 tonnes of CO₂ annually.

08
Statistics · Inferential Analysis · Fintech
Fintech Growth Analytics — A Statistical Deep Dive
Four statistical frameworks applied to a Nigerian digital lending dataset in one cohesive notebook. A/B hypothesis testing (did a new UI significantly improve conversion?), multiple linear regression with full assumptions testing (what predicts repayment?), Holt-Winters time series forecasting (Q2 disbursement projection with confidence intervals), and Chi-Square + ANOVA (are default rates significantly different across borrower tiers?). Both academic rigour and plain-English business interpretation throughout.
Python scipy.stats statsmodels A/B Testing OLS Regression Holt-Winters Chi-Square ANOVA
